Quinoa Poems

An appreciation post for one of my favorite foods

Quinoa

 

I'm keen on Quinoa

A texture so obscure and new.

A mystery, a feeling.

Like a sky so blue.

 

There's a translucent color.

A transparent taste.

Fine grain circles.

Never eaten in haste.

 

From the bowl of Zorro.

To the sands of the crisp.

The experiences feed into me

As I feed on it.
