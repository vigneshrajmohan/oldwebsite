Musical Tangents
September 18, 2017

My parents made me take piano lessons since I was 5 years old. The problem was that whenever I was told to practice, I would go on "musical tangents" and explore new variations on themes and melodies, etc. Composition and experimentation interested me way more than playing right out of the book. To this day, I randomly go on musical tangents that could last from a couple of seconds to as long as an hour. Only recently did I begin recording these moments, and I will try to post them here whenever I get the chance.
