My Favorite Movies

These are my top 15 favorite movies.

These movies are in no particular order. These movies are different in various aspects and shine in their own ways, so I can't properly rank them against eachother.

The Social Network

Directed by David Fincher

This movie has in a sense defined me as a person. There is a lot to learn from this movie about the world of the millenials. A new interest in coding and development sparked in me after watching this movie. Some say this movie defines our generation, and in several aspects I agree that it truly does. The quippy script artfully writted by Aaron Sorkin and the masterful directing by David Fincher made this movie what it is, and gave it the top spot on my list. Also, the soundtrack is fantastic, and I listen to it all the time.

"Let the hacking begin."

 

Whiplash

Directed by Damien Chazelle

This is a cool movie about dedication and hard work. Andrew disregards all of the nay-sayers and continues to push towards accomplishing his goal of becoming the best drummer the world has ever seen. It is quite the site, and the last 10 minutes of this movie are breathtaking. Also, great soundtrack.

"There are no two words in the English language more harmful than "good job"."
